Tutorial: Survive the Grid

1) Build Power first
Power strain is the heart of the game. Stay under ~85% load or volatility climbs fast.
2) Add Residential + Industry
Residential grows population (workforce). Industry earns credits but demands power + workforce.
3) Watch Stability & Volatility
Stability drops when pressure rises. Volatility is the real danger: events start to chain.
4) Services = shock absorbers
Hospitals reduce outbreak severity and speed recovery. Police reduce riot spread but can hurt long-term satisfaction if overused.
5) Expand sectors carefully
Unlocking new sectors increases complexity. Big systems become fragile if over-connected.
